What is a Hedge: Barrier

Q: How to set limits or boundaries in our walk with God.


Why do you go to certain individuals for advice?
They will tell you what they want you to hear.
"The truth shall set you free." - John 8:32

When you identify weaknesses, be able to discern them in order to put boundaries in
our lives to keep us away from our weaknesses.

Story of Job:
He had everything and the enemy takes it to the point where he has nothing. The enemy
had an intention of getting him to a bending point.
Three friends and his wife come to him with responses that were not positive, but blameful.
Job 1:9

Different Types of Hedges: Self set to protect us from your personal weaknesses, and some that
God places because he knows how we are each wired.

If your love for God is present, we must put up hedges in our lives to protect us and keep
us on God's path.
Ex. In work, make wise choices with free time, who you spend time alone with.
Make hedges by being accountability with a trusted person, decide how to spend
your free time, decide that you will not be alone with the opposite sex.

Our goal is to be with the Lord one day and there will be "valleys of deaths."

Q: How do you know which career path to choose?

As a man, God has called you to be the head of the home according to God's structure.
For Bro Saturn, he was stubborn in his vision for his Life. He really wanted to be a cop, like every
boy. He was dead on on what he did. He didn't seek for advice, nor did he seek God for God's will.

Red flags: Don't seek counsel from parents, experienced professionals in interested field, don't seek God's will.

God chooses different people for different things.
You can either A) Do it your way
B) Do it God's way // Slow down and do it God's way
God wants you to be with your family and around godly environments and focus on being a man/woman of God.

Wherever God places you: "Seek first the kingdom of God and His Righteousness." Matthew 6:33

Summary: 1.Set Boundaries and don't test God's placed boundaries.
2.Do it God's way; He will show you what to do.
