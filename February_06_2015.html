Friday February 6, 2015


Q: How to be ready for opposition? How to to talk about our faith to homosexuals, Muslims, agnostics, etc...

A: (Saturn)

Proverbs 1:7
-"The fear of the Lord is the beginning of knowledge:
but fools despise wisdom and instruction."
- it will give us confidence

Matthew 10:28
- "And fear not them which kill the body, but are not able to kill the soul:but rather
fear him which is able to destroy both soul and body in hell."
- don't fear what can/will happen to our physical being

Proverbs 14:26
- "In the fear of the Lord is strong confidence:
and his children shall have a place of refuge."
- we'll have strong confidence in what we believe, our faith, in the Word of God.

Fear the LORD, not what people will say/think about you.

Take a stand for the things of God with conviction, without being rude (use scripture)

Ecclesiastes 12:13
- "Let us hear the conclusion of the whole matter:Fear God, and keep his commandments:
for this is the whole duty of man."

Our actions (the manner we live) can speak louder than our words.

1 John 4:18
- "There is no fear in love; but perfect love casteth out fear:because fear hath torment.
He that feareth is not made perfect in love."

If our intentions are to put people down/in their place (to be confrontational), then keep your mouth shut.
You won't honor GOD with a spirit of pride.

- not just an intellectual fear, but also an actual trebling fear.

- "But the natural man receiveth not the things of the Spirit of God:for they are foolishness unto him:

- we must speak to others with love

in the world all around us (at school, work, store. Etc.)
