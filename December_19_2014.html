Social Media Recap

"All things are lawful, but not beneficial" 1 Corinthians 6:11-12
Lawful: Legal

"If your eye, hand, foot causes you or someone else to stumble, cast if off."
Eye: People you see
Hand: Things you do
Foot: Places you go.

Some people, things, and places become so attached, that they seem as vital as an eye, foot or hand. Not physically, of course, but as things that become a part of our lives.

If someone were to ask you to cut off your eye, hand, or foot would you consent? Absolutely not,
right? The only time we cut it off, is when there is cancer or some sort of illness. When it comes
down to making such a medical decision before the illness has spread, it is either your eye, hand, or foot
or your whole body will be affected. Similarly, if your hand is going to cause spiritual destruction,
then it is best to cut off entirely before it spreads.

THREE CHECK POINTS: Just because something is not bad, or is legal, doesn’t mean it is beneficial.
Check one: Is it lawful?
Check two: Is it beneficial to my ultimate goals, who I am now or who I want to become?
Check three: I will not allow myself to become controlled by these things (Exercise self disciple).

Ex: College student is going to take three classes, two have nothing to do with your major.
Is it a good class?
Are they going to help me reach my goal?
This will be a deal breaker.
- I will control myself and not take them because I know they do not align with my educational plan.

Most people don’t reach goals because they do what they WANT to do versus what they NEED to do. The same is true
with our spiritual walk with God. We need to do what we need, not what we want by looking at the three check points:
Is it lawful, is it beneficial, if not, I will exercise self discipline.

Music


A lot of people are under the power of worldly music. In other words, they have no control of their listening to it. Music is spirit, words are spirit, you can not see them. You can see the instrument, but not the tune.

Ezekiel: Music was created for two purposes: Worship God and stimulate others to worship God, not personal entertainment.

"Speaking to yourselves in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing and making melody in your heart to the Lord;" Ephesians 5:19

Any music that does not move you to draw closer to God, should not be listened to.

Lucifer was made with an instrumental body; instruments were built into his body.
Every time he moved and he opened his eyes, there was music.
The enemy takes everything that was made for God and twists it. People have done the same and made it for their
pleasure instead. Satan took what was meant to worship God to try and be like God. When we take things that
God made for him for ourselves, we are following Satan's footsteps.

A lot of musicians are living against God's word. You listening to them, you are being inspired by wicked hearts;
things that are corrupt; things that are against God’s word. By listening to these, you are robbing God's glory.
Words that are not from inspiration from God are death; like junk food, but for your ears.

Spiritual Hymns: Touch your spirit and connect your to God, everything else appeases the flesh, for YOUR own
pleasure, just like what Satan did.

What to do? Cut it off. If you can not let go, it is a sign that you have been taken under its power.
It hurts because it has become a part of your life, like and an arm, foot, eye.
