Lukewarmness theory
Someone once said that if they were born into a family that was
luke warm that the next generation would be warm,
then the next generation would be hot,
then the next generation would be on fire,
then the next generation would be nuclear.

When in reality the opposite was true.
If their family was lukewarm, then the next generation would be cool,
then the next generation would be cold,
then the next generation would be frozen.

The only one who can break these cycels of poor habits is the Lord's Spirit.

We need to be broken and HE is the only one that can break our genertational
cycles.

The Word of God is more than just black and white text: it is Spirit and it
is Truth. How many of us need His Spirirt and truth?
